mission "Sell Mereti Ships: Begin Sequence"
	landing
	invisible
	to offer
		has "event: wanderers: exiles have drones"
	on offer
		event "sell mereti ships: model 8 and model 16 available" 60 90
		fail

mission "Sell Mereti Ships: Model 8 and Model 16 Available"
	landing
	invisible
	source
		government "Wanderer" "Kor Mereti"
	to offer
		has "event: sell mereti ships: model 8 and model 16 available"
	on offer
		conversation
			`A Mereti Model 8 approaches your ship as you land, and spits out a datachip no larger than a marble. It appears to be a message from... somebody, but without plugging it into your ship it's hard to tell.`
			choice
				`	(Disregard the message.)`
				`	(Delay your trip to the spaceport to read the message.)`
					goto readmessage
			action
				event "sell mereti ships: model 8 and model 16 unavailable"
			`	You carefully place the data chip back into the Model 8's receptacle, and the drone uncertainly moves off.`
				decline
			label readmessage
			action
				event "sell mereti ships: model 32 available" 70 90
			`	You head back up the stairs of the <ship>, take the data ship into the control room, and insert it into a small card reader. The chip beeps and displays its message on the console. You read:`
			``
			`		CAPTAIN [HUMAN, FIRST NAME]:`
			`			FOR YOUR INFORMATION, WE WILL BEGIN SELLING YOU THE LATEST VERSION OF THE MODEL 8 AND THE MODEL 16. THIS TRUST IS NOT LIGHTLY GRANTED. PLEASE TAKE IT SERIOUSLY, AND DO REFRAIN FROM ATTACKING US.`
			``
			`			THE MERETI COLLECTIVE`
			``
			`	Evidently the Mereti now trust you enough to sell their technology to you.`
